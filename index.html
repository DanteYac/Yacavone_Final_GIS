What Is Geographic Information Systems?

Have you ever wondered how data is stored, managed and mapped?

Your answer is GIS or geographic information systems.

GIS is a data managment system which is used to analyze and map data. The link below will bring you to a website that goes into detail explaining what GIS is and what it can do. There are endless possibilities that GIS can accomplish and is a great major to investigate or learn how to use GIS programs. GIS is a rapidly growing field with jobs opening up daily.
